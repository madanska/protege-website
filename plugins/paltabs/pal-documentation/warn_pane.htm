The Constraint Warnings and Errors Pane

The Warnings and Errors pane at the right of the PAL Constraints Tab displays syntax validation warnings and errors for a selected constraint. Constraints which have warnings or errors are displayed with a red warning sign in the Status column after the Warn about indicated constraints button has been clicked in the Choose Constraints Pane.

This is an informational pane only. Clicking on another constraint will show the warnings for that constraint.

 

 

If the Evaluate indicated constraints button is pressed in the Choose Constraints Pane., the Warnings and Errors pane is replaced by the Query Responses Pane for Constraints.
