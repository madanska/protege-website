Validation Requirements for Student Rates

In order to qualify for the student rates, you must be a full-time student and be able to provide proof of your student status in the form of a copy of your student ID or a letter of acceptance to your academic program.

You are required to submit this information to us within one week of registering for the Protégé Conference. Please provide your verification documents via fax to the attention of Chris MacIntosh - Protégé Conference Registration. Our fax number is (650) 725-7944. Alternatively, if you have a copy of your letter of acceptance or photo ID in PDF format, you can email it to us as an attached file.
