Please read the following pertinent information before registering to attend the Protégé Conference.

Early Registration Deadline: June 9th, 2006

The conference early registration fee is $475.00 USD. For full-time students, the rate is $200.00 USD (please review the eligibility and validation requirements to ensure that you are eligible for this rate prior to registering). For full-time Stanford University staff the rate is $200.00 USD. After June 9th, the registration fees will be raised to $700.00 USD and $400 USD for students/staff respectively.

Payment may be made securely using our online registration site (see link below) by credit card or later by check, which will be due within 4 weeks of registration.

All cancellation requests must be received no later than July 14, 2006. Further cancellation instructions will be provided in your registration confirmation.

The following fees will apply for cancellations:
  • Registrants cancelling on or before June 9, 2006, will receive a refund of their registration fee, less a $50 processing fee.
  • Registrants cancelling after June 9, 2006 but before July 3, 2006 will forfeit 50% of their registration fee.
  • Registrants cancelling on or after July 3, 2006, will forfeit their entire registration fee.

Conference registrants who require invitation letters in order to process their visa requests should correspond with us via e-mail. Letters will only be sent to the individuals who have registered for the conference, and not to any third parties. Please note that no letters can be sent until after we have received and verified the payment of all registration fees. Please make requests early enough to allow time for delivery of our letter via Postal Service or FAX prior to scheduled interviews.

Still have questions about the registration process? Please contact the conference organizers.

Registering for the Protégé Conference implies permission to use any course-related photographs taken of you for publicity purposes.
