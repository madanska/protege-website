Conference Banquet: Tuesday, July 25th, 7:00-9:30PM

We are excited to announce that the Protégé Conference banquet will take place at Buca di Beppo restaurant, featuring "Immigrant Southern Italian" cuisine served family style in a festive atmosphere. The restaurant is located in down town Palo Alto - 643 Emerson Street at Forest (just under 2 blocks south of University Ave - across the street from Gordon Biersch). Phone # 650-329-0665

Details:
  • Banquet fare will consist of family style platters brought to your table including 2 antipasti, 2 salads, 2 pastas, 1 entree, 2 side dishes and 2 desserts. View the menu here.
  • Also included is your choice of beverage: non-alcoholic (soft drinks, coffee, tea - all you can drink) as well as one glass of house chianti.
  • Transportation to and from the conference venue is also included (for those staying at the Sheraton or SLAC Guest House - one shuttle will continue from the conference venue and take you to your hotels). For those who wish to drive (allow extra time to find street parking) here are:
    • Map of Buca Di Beppo
    • Driving Directions from Stock Farm Event Parking Structure to Buca Di Beppo
  • We have reserved private rooms with the dinner beginning at 7:00 pm and ending around 9:30 pm.
  • There is $50.00 USD charge for the banquet, which includes gratuities.
  • Attire is casual.

Please Note: You must register via our online form if you are interested in attending the banquet. There is a limit of 85 attendees and the deadline to register is Friday, July 21!
