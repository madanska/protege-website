Getting to Stanford:
  • By Air : Stanford University is located approximately half way between San Francsico International (SFO) and San Jose Mineta International (SJC). A third airport, Oakland International (OAK) is a bit further away. Follow the links above to your airport to learn more about ground transportation options (shuttles, taxis, public transportation).
  • By Car: Stanford can be reached via Highways 101 and 280 as well as the El Camino Real (82). See the section below on the conference venues for driving directions.

Getting to the Conference:

The conference will be held in 2 locations on the west side of campus in the Medical Center:

  • Sunday, 23 July: The first day of Tutorials/Workshop will be held at the James H Clark Center , located at 318 CAMPUS DR between Roth Way and Via Ortega . Check-in is between 8:00 and 9:00 AM in the Auditorium, located below the central courtyard formed by the 3 interconnected buildings of this center.
  • Monday - Wednesday, 24 - 26 July: The remainder of the conference will take place in the Sherman Fairchild Auditorium, 291 CAMPUS DR, between Via Ortega and Panama St. Those not attending the first day on Sunday should check-in at the registration table between8:00 and 9:00 AM located in the auditorium lobby

By Car:

Sunday, 23 July: You will be able to park in the large lot immediately adjacent to the Clark Center. Since it is a Sunday, you will NOT need a parking permit.

By Campus/Hotel Shuttles:

Many nearby hotels offer complimentary shuttle service to and from campus; inquire upon check in to your hotel for details.

The Marguerite: The University operates a free shuttle service that runs to most places on campus including our conference venue and several nearby places of lodging (SLAC Guest House, Sheraton/Westin, Stanford Terrace Inn) as well as the University and California Ave downtown areas. Look for the red and white buses.

  • Route maps, schedules and more information available here.
  • Map showing shuttle stops closest to the conference venues

N ote: on Sunday this shuttle normally does not operate. We have chartered a special charter bus to take attendees staying at the SLAC Guest House and the Sheraton/Westin to and from the conference venue using the following schedule:

Morning: Pick up in front of the Sheration at 7:20 am and then SLAC at 7:40 am to arrive at the Clark Center at 8:00 am.

Afternoon: Pick up at Clark Center at 5:45 pm to arrive at SLAC at 6:05 pm and then at the Sheraton at 6:25 pm

There is no advance reservation necessary, and this service is free of charge. Out of consideration of your fellow attendees and to ensure that you do not miss the shuttle, please be on time!
