Conference Banquet

Banquet registration is closed (as of Monday, June 28th)

We are excited to announce that the Protégé Conference banquet will take place during a cruise on the Potomac river. The cruise will provide a great opportunity to check out the Washington DC skyline.

Details:
  • Banquet fare will consist of a buffet-style dinner, coffee, tea, iced tea, and dessert.
  • A cash bar will be available for soda, juice, and alcoholic beverages.
  • A live DJ and cabaret-style show will provide entertainment during dinner.
  • The $50.00 charge for the banquet includes gratuities.
  • Cruise attire should be casual to dressy.
Boarding will begin at 6:30PM at Pier 4 at 6th & Water Streets SW, Washington DC. The pier is an easy five minute walk from the Waterfront Station on the Green Line Metro. Comprehensive directions to the pier are available online. The ship will depart at 7:00PM and return to port at 10:00PM.

We hope to see all of you at the banquet!
