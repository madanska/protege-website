Workshop on Medical Applications of Protégé

Tuesday, July 6th, 2004 at 1:30 to 5:30
Bethesda, Maryland

In conjunction with the Seventh International Protégé Conference

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners with an interest in using Protégé in medical applications. The workshop format is a series of presentations followed by a general discussion.
