The following workshops were accepted after our Call for Workshops. Please note that this is a tentative list and is subject to change.

  • Ontology Transformation and Interoperability (Harold Solbrig, Thomas Johnson, Christopher Chute)
    This workshop will focus on the methodologies, tooling and approaches that can be used to increase the likelihood that Protégé-authored ontologies can be reused.
  • Protégé with Rules (Christine Golbreich)
    The goal of the workshop is to bring together researchers and users interested in extensions to the Protégé OWL Plugin and various aspects of reasoning in the Protégé environment.
