Registration Cancellation Policy

All cancellations must be received no later than July 8, 2005. You can submit your cancellation either via email, or in writing addressed to:

Attn: Ted Hopper
M.S.O.B., X-215
251 Campus Dr.
Stanford, CA 94305-5479
USA

The following fees will apply for cancellations:

  • Registrants cancelling on or before June 2, 2005, will receive a refund of their registration fees, less a $50 processing fee.
  • Registrants cancelling after June 2, 2005 but before June 28, 2005, will receive a refund equal to 40% of their registration fees.
  • Registrants cancelling after July 8, 2005, will forfeit their entire registration fee.
