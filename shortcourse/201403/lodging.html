Please note that we qualify for special discount room rates at several local hotels listed below. If none of the listed hotels meet your needs, please refer to Stanford's excellent visitor information services site for alternative lodging options.

If you make a reservation at any of the hotels listed below, please identify yourself as a Protégé Short Course attendee.


Cardinal Hotel (website: http://goo.gl/ProQu)
The historic Cardinal Hotel is situated in the heart of downtown Palo Alto, within walking distance of many restaurants, cafes, bookstores, retails shops, art galleries, and theaters. The hotel is 5 blocks from the Palo Alto Transit Center where Stanford's complimentary public Marguerite shuttle runs to and from campus. Downtown Palo Alto parking permits are 15.00 USD/day.

Rooms held through March 1, 2014

Rates: 109.00 USD/night (shared bath), 189.00 USD/night (private bath)


Stanford Guest House (website: http://goo.gl/Rce2r)
Nestled in the scenic Palo Alto foothills on the grounds of the SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory, the Stanford Guest House is owned and operated by Stanford and was designed specifically with university guests in mind. The Guest House is located approximately 3 miles from the Stanford campus. Stanford's complimentary public Marguerite shuttle runs between the Guest House and campus from 7:30AM - 8:00PM.

Rooms held through February 23, 2014

Online registration instructions: http://goo.gl/y8ltMP

 Rates: 134.00 USD/night


Creekside Inn (website: http://goo.gl/mLYqq)
The Creekside Inn is a boutique hotel in a creekside garden setting, located approximately 3 miles from both downtown Palo Alto and the Stanford Campus. Parking is available on-site and the hotel provides free hybrid car service to and from Stanford University. Amenities include an outdoor pool, fitness center, restaurant, complimentary wireless, and continental breakfast.

Rooms held through March 5, 2014

Rates: 169.00 USD/night


Stanford Terrace Inn (website: http://goo.gl/cavxS)
The Stanford Terrace Inn is a green hotel located in a secluded garden setting across the street from the Stanford campus. The inn offers complimentary shuttle service to and from Stanford, covered parking, continental breakfast, outdoor pool, workout room, complimentary wireless, and allergy free green suites.

Rooms held through February 28, 2014

Rates: 189.00 USD/night (single queen), 199.00 USD/night (double queen)
